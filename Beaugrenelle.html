Description

Situated opposite the Seine, in the Beaugrenelle district in the 15th arrondissement, Beaugrenelle Paris is a shopping centre with a surface area of 45,000 m². Built at the end of the 1970s, the building reopened in October 2013 after huge renovation work. It houses a hundred or so fashion, beauty, home, and leisure brands including a large Marks and Spencer department store, 10 restaurants, a 10-screen cinema and a car park with 1,100 parking spaces. The green rooftop is home to beehives and a garden designed to be a habitat for birds.


Opening times

Shops are open from Monday to Saturday from 10am to 8.30pm and on sundays from 11am to 7pm. The cinema and restaurants are open Monday to Sunday from 9.30am to midnight.
