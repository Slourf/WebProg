Description

The Forum des Halles has 150 stores with 35 new establishments: exclusive brands (L'Exception, Sept-Cinq, What For, Herschel ...), the flagship stores of the greatest French and international brands (Nike, Lego, Superdry, Muji ...) a strong catering service with 17 restaurants.
The centre also renovated 70% of its existing space, with extensive restructuring operations for many brands such as Fnac, Zara, San Marina, Celio, Bershka. Finally, convenience is added with a 3,000m² Monoprix and a Causses grocery shop.
The Forum des Halles is also a leisure destination, with the busiest cinema in Europe and its historical equipment (Forum des images, gymnasium, Olympic swimming pool). With the Canopy, the cultural offer is enriched with a media library, a centre dedicated to hip-hop, a conservatory and the Maison des pratiques artistiques amateurs.
