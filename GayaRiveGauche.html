Description

One name: Pierre Gagnaire. One food: fish. With its simple and incredibly classy style, Gaya Rive Gauche is dedicated to the enjoyment of seafood: crab, salmon, anchovies, sea bream, etc. All the fish and other shellfish are expertly cooked by the chef with new and complex flavours. The restaurant also impresses with the freshness of the products and the great dessert and wine menus.
